Fuel Your Body Right

Discover the finest protein sources curated for both vegetarians and non-vegetarians. Build muscle, recover faster, and live healthier.

Vegetarian Sources

Plant-based

Lentils

18g / cup

A nutritional powerhouse packed with protein, fiber, and essential minerals. Perfect for soups, stews, and salads.

Plant-based

Tofu (Firm)

20g / half-cup

Made from soybeans, it's a complete protein containing all nine essential amino acids. Highly versatile in cooking.

Dairy

Greek Yogurt

15g / 6 oz

Thick, creamy, and loaded with casein protein and gut-friendly probiotics. Great for breakfast or snacks.

Dairy

Cottage Cheese

14g / half-cup

Rich in slow-digesting casein protein, making it an excellent late-night snack for muscle recovery.

Plant-based

Quinoa

8g / cup

A gluten-free seed that serves as a complete protein. An excellent alternative to rice or pasta.

Plant-based

Edamame

17g / cup

Young soybeans that are delicious steamed and salted. A perfect high-protein snack.

Non-Vegetarian Sources

Meat

Chicken Breast

31g / 3 oz

The gold standard for lean muscle building. Highly versatile and extremely low in fat when skinless.

Poultry/Egg

Whole Eggs

6g / large egg

Nature's multivitamin. Boasts the highest biological value of protein, meaning your body absorbs it perfectly.

Seafood

Salmon

22g / 3 oz

Not just great for protein, but loaded with heart-healthy Omega-3 fatty acids that reduce inflammation.

Meat

Lean Beef

26g / 3 oz

Packed with highly bioavailable iron, zinc, and vitamin B12 alongside premium quality protein.

Seafood

Tuna (Canned)

20g / 3 oz

A convenient, budget-friendly source of extremely lean protein. Perfect for sandwiches and salads.

Meat

Turkey Breast

25g / 3 oz

Similar to chicken breast but with a slightly different flavor profile. Extremely lean and protein-dense.
